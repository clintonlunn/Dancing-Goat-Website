About Us

Our mission is to provide the most high quality, delicious, and healthy products while showing the individual love and care that each animal deserves.

Quality is paramount and we personally stand behind our products.

We are committed to growing a relationship with our customers and educating the next generation in responsible agriculture.

Our hearts are set on sustainability and creative green innovation that eliminates waste.”

A refugee from Corporate America, Pam started The Dancing Goat in August of 2007. First selling to friends and family and by word of mouth, she expanded to Farmer’s Markets and added in eggs from free range chickens and artisan goat milk soap. She has been an active vendor at Saturday Morning Market in St. Petersburg and Sweetwater Organic Farm Sunday Market for over 11 years. It isn’t unusual to find her at market with the newest born baby goats so folks can experience a little “piece of the farm.

Driven by the self-proclaimed yuppie turned farmer, Pam’s company won Creative Loafing’s “Loafie” award for Best Raw Goat Milk in Tampa Bay for 2008, the Cordon D’or International Culinary Academy Great Tastes of Florida Award 2011 and the Florida Farm Award for 2013, Pam served as Youth Dairy Goat Superintendent for the Florida State Fair for over 15 years and mentor/advisor to 4H, FFA and youth in the area. The Dancing Goat is currently a partnership facility with St. Petersburg College Veterinary Science Program and Pam sits on the Advisory Board for the Tarpon Springs High School Veterinary Academy. She maintains a herd of competition dairy goats which have taken numerous Grand Champion awards in Florida and Georgia. The Dancing Goat is a family business with husband, Jim being an integral part of the business.

Recycle, Reuse and Repurpose is the farm motto!

The goats at The Dancing Goat Dairy live life in luxury! They are fed the best hay money can buy and a custom mixed grain on the milking stand in minimal amounts.
